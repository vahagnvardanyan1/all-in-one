## Confirmed Events in Armenia

### 1. Second Investment and Financial Forum of Armenia

- **Date:** March 3, 2026
- **Location:** Dvin Music Hall, Yerevan
- **Website:** [forum.ffin.am/en](https://forum.ffin.am/en/)
- **Type:** International investment and financial forum
- **Theme:** "The Moment of Opportunity: Armenia in the New Architecture of Global Markets"
- **Investors:** Yes — capital markets, investment instruments, financial institutions
- **Registration:** Open at forum.ffin.am
- **Organized by:** Freedom Broker Armenia
- **Topics:** Capital markets, investment instruments, digital transformation of finance, human capital
- **Relevance:** Good for meeting investors interested in fintech and digital transformation

### 2. IV Conference Capital Markets Armenia

- **Date:** March 13, 2026
- **Location:** Armenia Marriott Hotel, 1 Amiryan Street, Yerevan
- **Website:** [cbonds-congress.com](https://cbonds-congress.com/events/1365/)
- **Type:** International finance and investment conference
- **Investors:** Yes — regulators, banks, investment companies, international financial institutions, rating agencies. 240+ participants from 12 countries in 2025.
- **Topics:** Armenia's economy, investment attractiveness, global financial markets, **Digital Finance, AI & Market Infrastructure** (dedicated session)

- **Date:** July 26 — August 1, 2026
- **Location:** Sevan Festival City, Lake Sevan, Armenia
